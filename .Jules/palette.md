## 2024-11-20 - AIChatAssistant Accessibility Improvements\n**Learning:** The AIChatAssistant component previously lacked critical accessibility features, making it difficult for screen reader users to understand the purpose of its icon-only buttons. It also lacked visual focus indicators for keyboard users.\n**Action:** Applied aria-label to all icon-only interactive elements and added focus-visible utility classes to ensure clear keyboard navigation visibility.
